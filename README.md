Title: AI Meal Planner – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/29/2025

This privacy policy explains how AI Meal Planner (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and the associated web app at https://monesh-ai-meal-planner.streamlit.app/.
By using AI Meal Planner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

Email address
Username
Password (stored in hashed form using bcrypt – we never store your raw password)
Usage and Input Data

Ingredients and dietary preferences you enter
Meal plans generated for you
Calorie goals and other options you provide
Time and date when you generate or save a plan
Technical Information

Basic technical information sent by your device or browser (such as IP address, browser type, and device information), as needed for security and service operation.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

Authenticate and manage your user account
Generate personalized meal plans using AI
Save your meal plan history so you can review it later
Improve and maintain our services
Protect against misuse or abuse of the service
3. Third‑Party Services
We use the following third‑party services to operate AI Meal Planner:

OpenAI

We send your inputs (ingredients, dietary preferences, and related text) to the OpenAI API in order to generate meal plans.
OpenAI may process this data according to its own privacy policy.
MongoDB (Database)

We store account information (username, email, hashed password) and saved meal plans in a MongoDB database.
This data is used to authenticate you and to show your history.
Google Sheets (Optional Storage)

In some cases, meal plans may be stored in a Google Sheets document for backup or analytics.
The saved data includes timestamp, user ID/username, calorie goal, ingredient inputs, and the generated plan text.
We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing and are never saved in plain text.
Access to databases and APIs is restricted by secret keys and environment variables.
We use HTTPS where available to protect data in transit.
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Children’s Privacy
AI Meal Planner is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

7. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

Access the personal data we hold about you
Request correction of inaccurate data
Request deletion of your data
Object to or restrict certain processing
You can contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
